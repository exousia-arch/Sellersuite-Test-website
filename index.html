Questions

Is PPCBench really free?

It's free for approved testers during the private beta. Pricing will be introduced later — beta users will get advance notice.

Why do I need to request access?

We're onboarding testers gradually to gather focused feedback before opening up. Request access and we'll send your login if it's a fit.

Can you see my campaign data?

No. Your bulk files and reports are processed entirely in your browser and are never uploaded to us. See our Privacy Policy.

Which marketplaces are supported?

All of them. PPCBench recognizes localized Amazon column headers and number/currency formats (US, EU, JP, and more), and the interface is available in several languages.

Is this an official Amazon tool?

No. PPCBench is independent and not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Amazon.
